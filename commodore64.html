The Legendary Commodore 64

History

Commodore 64, abbreviated C64, was introduced in 1982 and gained worldwide popularity.
It is still the most sold computer in the world.

Current Status

There's still a wide community of C64 enthusiasts. There are numerous C64 emulators,
such as CCS64 (link to http://www.ccs64.com/) and Frodo (link to http://frodo.cebix.net/).
